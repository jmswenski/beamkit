# BeamKit Disclaimer

BeamKit is early-stage, open-source research and workflow software for radiation oncology informatics.

BeamKit is not a medical device, is not FDA-cleared, and is not approved by any regulatory authority for diagnosis, treatment planning, treatment delivery, quality assurance, or clinical decision-making.

Do not use BeamKit as the sole basis for clinical decisions. Any use in a clinical environment must be independently validated by qualified professionals under applicable institutional policies, regulatory requirements, and quality-management procedures.

BeamKit examples, samples, tests, and documentation use synthetic data only. Do not contribute protected health information, patient identifiers, proprietary treatment-planning-system files, or proprietary SDK binaries to this repository.
